# Code of Conduct

SafeClaw follows the Contributor Covenant version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- being respectful in all discussions
- staying focused on safety and technical quality
- accepting feedback constructively
- reporting potential security issues responsibly

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- discrimination or exclusion
- publishing private or sensitive security details without permission

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing standards and will take
appropriate action, including warnings or removal from the project.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to the repository maintainers listed in `SECURITY.md`.
All reports are confidential and reviewed promptly.
